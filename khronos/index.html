The Khronos chamber serves as a historical archive tracking the evolution and key milestones of the ETCETER4 project. Navigate between eras (major time periods) and milestones (specific events) to explore the project's chronological development. Integration with D3.js and vis.js provides interactive timeline visualizations and temporal data exploration.
